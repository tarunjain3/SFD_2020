Software Freedom Day!

Software Freedom Day is a global celebration of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use.
Ever since itsinception, SFD has grown in scale and participation at Panjab University. Our past speakers have been from a multitude of fields and various organizations like Mozilla, Facebook, Microsoft and Google, just to name a few. SFD aims to be an accessible and community-driven conference, and be open to developers and students of all levels.
